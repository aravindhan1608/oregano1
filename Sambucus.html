USES OF SAMBUCUS NIGRA

1.ANTIVIRAL ACTIVITY

Studies have shown that elderberry extracts may have antiviral effects against certain strains of influenza viruses and other respiratory viruses. Elderberry compounds inhibit viral replication and may help reduce the severity and duration of viral infections when taken at the onset of symptoms.

2.RESPIRATORY HEALTH

Elderberry has traditionally been used to alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections, such as coughs, congestion, and sore throat. Elderberry extracts or syrups are believed to have anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties that can help soothe respiratory symptoms and promote faster recovery from respiratory illnesses.

3.COLD AND FLU RELIEF

Elderberry products are commonly used as natural remedies for colds, flu, and other viral infections. Elderberry syrup or capsules are often taken orally to relieve symptoms such as fever, cough, congestion, and body aches. Some research suggests that elderberry supplementation may shorten the duration of cold and flu symptoms and reduce the need for over-the-counter medications.

4.ANTIOXIDANT PROTECTION

Elderberries are one of the richest sources of plant-based antioxidants, which help protect cells from damage caused by oxidative stress and inflammation. Regular consumption of elderberry products may help reduce the risk of chronic diseases associated with oxidative damage, such as heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.

5.SKIN HEALTH

Elderberry extracts are sometimes used in skincare products for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Elderberry oil or extract may help soothe irritated skin, reduce redness and inflammation, and protect against environmental damage. Elderberry-based skincare products are used to hydrate, nourish, and rejuvenate the skin, promoting a healthy and youthful complexion.
