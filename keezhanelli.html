USES OF KEEZHANELLI

1.HEALTH

Keezhanelli is often used to support liver health. It is believed to have hepatoprotective properties, which means it helps protect the liver from damage caused by toxins or diseases.


2.KIDNEY HEALTH

In traditional medicine, Keezhanelli is also used to support kidney health and treat various kidney disorders. It is believed to help in the management of conditions like kidney stones and urinary tract infections.


3.DIGESTIVE HEALTH

Keezhanelli is used to aid digestion and treat gastrointestinal disorders like indigestion, constipation, and stomach ache.


4.ANTIOXIDANT PROPERTIES

The plant contains antioxidants that help in combating oxidative stress and reducing inflammation in the body.


5.DIURETIC EFFECTS

It is believed to have diuretic properties, promoting urine production and aiding in the elimination of toxins from the body.
