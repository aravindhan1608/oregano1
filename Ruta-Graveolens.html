Ruta Graveolens


USES OF Ruta Graveolens

1.Anti-inflammatory

Rue contains compounds with anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful in reducing inflammation associated with conditions such as arthritis, rheumatism, and gout.

2.Antispasmodic

The herb has been used traditionally to relieve muscle spasms and cramps. It may be beneficial for conditions such as muscle cramps, menstrual cramps, and spasmodic coughs.

3.Topical Applications

Rue has been used topically in various forms, including poultices and ointments, to treat skin conditions such as bruises, sprains, strains, and insect bites. It is believed to have analgesic and anti-inflammatory effects when applied externally.

4.Abortifacient

Historically, rue has been used as an emmenagogue (to stimulate menstruation) and an abortifacient (to induce abortion). However, its use for these purposes is highly discouraged due to safety concerns, as rue contains compounds that can be toxic and potentially harmful, especially in high doses.

5.Traditional Spiritual and Folk Uses

Rue has also been used in various cultures for spiritual and folkloric purposes. It has been associated with protection, purification, and warding off evil spirits. Rue was sometimes worn as an amulet or carried in sachets for its purported protective properties.
