USES OF CHAMOMILE

1.RELAXATION AND STRESS RELIEF

Chamomile is well-known for its calming effects. Drinking chamomile tea or using chamomile essential oil in aromatherapy can help reduce stress and promote relaxation.

2.IMPROVING SLEEP

Due to its calming effects, chamomile tea is often consumed before bedtime to aid in better sleep. It can help alleviate insomnia and promote restful sleep

3.DIGESTIVE HEALTH

Chamomile has been traditionally used to relieve digestive issues such as indigestion, gas, bloating, and stomach cramps. Drinking chamomile tea after meals may help soothe the digestive system.

4.ANTI-INFLAMMATORY PROPERTIES

Chamomile contains compounds with anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful for relieving inflammation and discomfort associated with conditions like arthritis and muscle soreness. It can be applied topically or consumed as a tea.

5.SKINCARE

Chamomile is gentle on the skin and has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it beneficial for various skin conditions. It can help soothe irritation, reduce redness, and promote healing. Chamomile tea can also be used as a natural facial toner or added to bathwater for a calming soak.
